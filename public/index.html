Gramaphone Records

Gramaphone Records is a Chicago musical institution, a central hub of the city's dance music scene that's been in business more than 50 years.

I've had the honor of working at the shop since 2011. I started by pricing records and working the register and eventually worked my way up to be the techno buyer, a position I held for a few years.

Over the years I've had the opportunity to create branding, printed materials, apparel, and artwork for the shop’s record label.

Stripped & Chewed

Stripped & Chewed is a house-music record label I started with some of my best friends in Chicago. We press vinyl records and always lovingly hand stamp and block print our releases.

I developed our logo and branding and over the years have created countless designs for flyers, stickers, pins and more.
